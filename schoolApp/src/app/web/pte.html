The Pearson Test of English Academic (PTE Academic) is an English language test designed to assess the readiness of non-native English speakers to participate in a university-level English language instruction programme. PTE Academic is the English test trusted by universities, colleges and governments around the world. You can take PTE Academic test to prove your English ability for university and college admissions or as part of a visa application for Australia.


Who should take PTE Academic?

Students and professionals who want to go to countries like Australia, Canada, etc. can take PTE Academic exam. Student would need to pass PTE Academic exam to be considered by foreign universities. Required score differs from university to university. Professionals would need to get the required score in this exam so that they can apply for visa and for jobs. Apart from this, spouses of test takers are also required to take the exam as mandated by the rules.
